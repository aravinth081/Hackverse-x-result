How is the HACKVERSE X certificate verified?

+
Every digital certificate generated on this portal is signed and contains a cryptographic Verification Code on the footer. This code registers your participation in our official database, allowing tech recruiters and organizations to verify your achievement natively.

I won one of the main prizes. How do I claim it?

+
Winners of the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd prizes receive an exclusive winner access code from the organizers. Navigate to the Winner Certificate Portal to verify your code, enter your details, and claim your YouCam API credits along with your official Prize Certificate.

What formats can I download the certificate in?

+
You can download your high-resolution certificate directly as a high-fidelity PNG image (ideal for LinkedIn, portfolios, and Twitter showcase) or as an A4 landscape print-ready PDF document.

What should I do if my name is misspelled or my certificate fails to generate?

+
Only registered participants, verified judges, and official award winners whose details are in our database can generate certificates. If your name is misspelled or you encounter generation errors, please contact the support team with your registration credentials.
